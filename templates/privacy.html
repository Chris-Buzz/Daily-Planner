1. Introduction

Welcome to Planno. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our daily planning application and services.

Planno ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all users of the Planno application, whether accessed via web, mobile, or desktop platforms.

By using Planno, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

Important Note

We recommend reading this Privacy Policy in its entirety to understand how your data is handled. If you have questions, please contact us at privacy@planno.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services to you.

2.1 Account Information

When you create a Planno account, we collect:

  • Email address (required for authentication and account recovery)
  • Display name or full name (for personalization)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Account preferences and settings

2.2 Planning and Task Data

To provide our core planning services, we store:

  • Tasks, to-do items, and their descriptions
  • Task metadata (creation date, due dates, completion status, priorities)
  • Schedules, calendar events, and time blocks
  • Categories, tags, and organizational preferences
  • Notes, comments, and task descriptions you create
  • Recurring task patterns and templates

2.3 Communication Preferences

If you enable notifications, we collect:

  • Email notification preferences
  • Preferred notification times and delivery methods
  • Push notification tokens for mobile devices

2.4 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about how you use Planno:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, browser type)
  • IP address and general location data (country/region level only)
  • Login timestamps and session duration
  • Feature usage patterns (which features you use most)
  • Error logs and diagnostic information

2.5 AI Assistant Interactions

When you use our AI planning assistant:

  • Your queries and requests to the AI assistant
  • AI-generated suggestions and responses
  • Interaction patterns to improve AI performance (anonymized)

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Enterprise-Grade Security

Your data is protected using the same security standards employed by major technology companies, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

4.1 Encryption

  • In Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (Transport Layer Security)
  • At Rest: Data stored in our databases is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Passwords: User passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt

4.2 Infrastructure Security

  • Hosted on Google Firebase with enterprise-level security certifications
  • Regular security patches and updates
  • Distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) protection
  • Automated backup systems with redundancy
  • Network isolation and firewall protection

4.3 Access Controls

  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) for team members
  • Comprehensive audit logs of all data access
  • Zero-knowledge architecture where feasible
  • Regular access reviews and permission audits

4.4 Data Isolation

Your data is logically isolated from other users' data. Only you can access your personal tasks and planning information through your authenticated account.

Your Responsibility

Please keep your password secure and do not share your account credentials. You are responsible for all activities that occur under your account. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

We only keep your data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

8.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services.

8.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile information is immediately removed from active systems
  • All tasks and planning data are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes
  • We may retain certain information if required by law (e.g., tax records, fraud prevention)

8.3 Inactive Accounts

If your account remains inactive for 3 years, we will send you a notification. If you don't respond within 90 days, we may delete your account and associated data.
